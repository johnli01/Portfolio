About Original Personal Statement

With this paper, I felt that I tried too hard in making it unique and quirky. Rereading this only made me cringe and fueled my desire in creating a better draft. It was not indepth enough nor did it reveal much about myself. The event did happen but now I do not believe that this event has been a contributing moment in finding my identity.

About Final Personal Statement

With this last version, I do believe I tried to summarize everything into one. 'Hide & Seek' was a fun and meaningful game to me during my childhood. I would play this game with friends and family members and had a blast. I just had to recall why I loved it, which once I answered, made it easy to connect to my life currently. It has taught me that valuable lesson in which is ingrained in my head to this day. The game is definitely a contributing factor in finding my identity.
